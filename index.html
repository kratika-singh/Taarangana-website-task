Taarangana is the annual cultural fest of IGDTUW

We unveil a new identity, Taarangana. Taarangana - Taaron ka Aangan, the trail of stars. Different colours represent the variety of backgrounds and genres coming together in this cultural blast. Let the stars guide you.

Taarangana 2020

In a myriad of art we got lost in the colours of thrill and enthusiasm! Dancing to the beats and moving in sync with the music, we forgot the monotony of life outside Taarangana. Wanting to relive the amazing time listening to Darshan Raval, laughing with Rahul dua & Sahil Shah, and dancing on the beats of dj vanmoon by VH1 Supersonic and tunes of dj moctave, here we are with the official aftermovie of Taarangana'20!
